Back in Stock: SMS Setup

What?

When enabled, the sign-up form will include the option for the user to include their mobile number as well as the email address.

When the product is restocked, the customer will receive the notification type they opt in for, Email, SMS or Web-push.


Why?

When enabled - SMS notifications on average convert at 3x the rate of email notifications. Prioritize SMS to ramp up your sales!

Pricing

SMS notifications will cost $0.015 per message. SMS is not currently not included in any of our monthly plans, so all SMS notifications will be charged in addition to your plan. Usage charges will appear on the same Shopify invoice as your monthly payment.


How to Setup


First, Enable SMS Notifications

(Shortcut to SMS Notifications page)


  1. Go to Back in Stock Alerts -> Configuration

2. Under tabs look for Notification Channels

  1. Enable both checkboxes and hit Save changes.

You will see the boxes get greyed out with a notice that SMS approval is underway.

You may wish to reach out to our support to get your request reviewed faster. Our team approves requests on average within 1 business day.

4.After it has been approved on our end, you may wish to return to confirm the notice is gone. SMS should be enabled automatically already.


Customizing SMS Template


  1. You can now adjust your SMS template via visiting this page

Customization -> Notification Templates -> SMS (tab). Shortcut to SMS Template

The SMS message has a maximum of 160 characters, the SMS message will need to be tailored to fit inside this limit.

The template requires that the shop.name, product.title, variant.url are present in the template.

The shop.name is your stores name.

The product/variant.title is the title of the product. You will generally want these next to each other, as if the product has variants the variant title may be used.

The variant.url is a link directly to the product.

There are also some optional variables, like the variant.price; this will print the product/variants price in the SMS.

For a full technical breakdown of All variables types, refer to this indepth article.

FAQ

Can I turn SMS notifications on for existing notifications?

No. Existing notifications were created with the customer's email address and a phone number is required to send it by SMS.

Can we send SMS notifications to customers in any country?

SMS Notifications can be sent to customers in any country, but they are currently only available for stores using the Latin/Roman alphabet in SMS messages.

Can I test out SMS notifications and then turn it off at will?

Sure, try it out and see how it goes! You can always turn it off if its not working for you. If SMS feature is enabled and then disabled, however, any pending notifications that haven't yet been sent will not send when your product is back in stock. This is because if a user opts for SMS notifications (not email) we wont have a way to contact them if SMS feature is turned off.

How do I see how many Ive sent? (to gauge popularity or cost)

Your account's Notification Report will show all notifications created with a phone number and exactly when it was sent.

Can we use our existing Short code for SMS delivery?

It is not possible to use a custom Short code at this time.

Can we include a product image in the SMS message?

No; at this time the message is text only.

Removed/Disallowed words;

In compliance with S.H.A.F.T the following words are automatically removed from any SMS message; Vape/Vaping, CBD/Marijuana/Cannabis.
