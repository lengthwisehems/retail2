Back in Stock: Getting Started & Setup Guide

Get your Back in Stock notifications running in 5 quick steps.

Questions? Reach out via Help button at the bottom right OR Email us at backinstock-support@useamp.com

Upon Install

Complete Welcome Form - Fill in your details and click "Get Started"

Choose Your Plan - Select the plan that fits your notification volume - All plans include every feature with a 14-day free trial

Setup Guide

Enable App Embed

  • Click "Enable in theme editor" to open Shopify's theme settings
  • Simply click "Save" in the theme editor (opens in new tab)
  • Return to Back in Stock app to continue



Turn Back in Stock On

This will navigate you through steps to setup Back in Stock button on your page.


5 Step Process:

1. Click "Enable Back in Stock" to activate the "Notify Me when Back in Stock" button

2. Click "Activate product page button" to enable buttons on product pages

3. Test your button - Click "See it live on your store" to view on actual products.

If no sold-out products exist, we'll create a test preview for you to see how it works

Advanced Back in Stock Setup


For more customisation and in depth options, we created an Advanced Back in Stock Setup Guide on Back in Stock Alerts > Overview page



This includes the ability to

Customization Option Description
Customize your signup form Design the popup form customers see when signing up
Customize your notification channels Enable SMS and web push notifications here
Customize your notifications sequence Create campaigns and follow-up messages (emails/SMS)
Customize your notifications Personalize email templates and content
Customize your floating button Position and style the side widget button
Customize your collections page button Add buttons to category/collection pages
Customize your delivery settings Control notification timing and prevent floods
