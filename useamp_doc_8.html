Back in Stock: Setup email reporting to your inbox

How to Setup Email Reporting


Automated Summary Reports

Receive daily or weekly performance overviews delivered to your inbox.

Setup Instructions:

  1. Go to Settings → Activity updates tab
  2. In "Summary reports" section:
    • Check "Subscribe to summary reports"
    • Set Email schedule: Daily or Weekly
    • Add recipients in "Deliver to" field
    • Choose time period: All time, or 1/7/30/90 days

Note: Reports are only sent when there's activity to report.


Immediate Alerts

Get real-time notifications for new customer signups.

Setup Instructions:

  1. Go to Settings → Activity updates tab
  2. In "Immediate alerts" section:
    • Add recipient email addresses in "Deliver to" field
    • Set maximum emails per day (default: 20, max: 100)
    • Click Save changes

Multiple Recipients: Separate email addresses with commas Example: sales@store.com, manager@store.com
