Everyday Style Redefined by Denim Innovation and Flawless Fit

For over 20 years, Joe’s Jeans has been leading the denim industry with jeans that are inherently LA with a distinct point of view. Our legendary collection of women’s jeans and men’s jeans come in many fits, from classic straight legs to on-trend wide legs – but denim is only the beginning.

There’s a Perfect Pair of Jeans for Everyone

No matter what your denim style is, we have a flattering fit for you. From curve-hugging skinny jeans to relaxed straight leg jeans, there are so many comfortable fits made in our premium denim. We even have shorter denim inseams for him and petite denim sizes for her.

Our Bestselling Denim Comes in Many Fits and Styles

  • Petite: Designed for women 5’4” and below, our collection of petite jeans is made with shorter inseams for the perfect fit. Our petite styles are available in a range of fits, from flared jeans to wide leg jeans to relaxed, loose fit jeans.
  • Straight: Our straight leg jeans for men and women are closet classics you’ll wear for years to come, and they are available in a variety of styles and denim washes.
  • Wide Leg: We have a large collection of trending denim fits, like wide leg jeans for women and men. From wide leg trouser jeans to relaxed wide leg jeans, we have the latest denim fits you want.
  • Boyfriend: Our relaxed slim jeans and boyfriend jeans are easy fits that style effortlessly year-round. The laid-back fit is perfect for dressing up with a blazer or dressing down with a casual t-shirt.

Finish Your Look with Denim-Ready Essentials

Besides perfect-fitting jeans for men and women, we have designed all the classic tops, t-shirts and tanks, and sweaters to complete your look. Dress up your denim with dressy button downs and pull-over sweaters, or go casual in timeless t-shirts and tank tops for weekends, vacation and more. We also have a collection of non-denim styles, like twill and linen pants for men and women, dresses and jackets.
